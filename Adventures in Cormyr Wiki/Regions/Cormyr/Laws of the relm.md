The following laws are posted at all major entry points into Cormyr:

- All persons entering Cormyr must register with the officials of a border garrison.  
- Foreign currency may only be used in designated locations. Exchange foreign coin for Cormyrean golden lions at your earliest opportunity.  
- Adventuring groups must possess a valid charter before undertaking any operations.  
   
All weapons must be peace-bonded. Exceptions apply only to:  

- Chartered adventuring groups  
- Mercenary companies with proof of employment  
- Harming cats is strictly forbidden.  
- Bow your head to royalty and local nobility.  
- Purple Dragons may search your person and belongings upon request.  
- Hunting on private land is forbidden.

Ignorance of the law is not a defense.
